Owner's Spotlight

The idea behind Black & Owning It came about in 2019. My partner and I were visiting a pop up shop and while supporting the young black entrepreneurs we realized that so many people were missing out on incredible talent and products due to lack of promotion. We wanted to create one common space where you can find these small businesses and help support them with the click of a button. Thus, Black & Owning It was born.
